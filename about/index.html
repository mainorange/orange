Multi-chain Investments, all routed back to Polygon

All of our investments are spread across several chains, including Polygon, Optimism, Avalanche, and Algorand. All the profits are routed back to Polygon, converted to stablecoins and returned to our holders.

Why choose our NFT over a Token?

Air Dropped Rewards
No need to claim rewards or stake your earnings. All rewards are airdropped to NFT holder's wallets without any action needed from the holder!
Cold-Wallet Compatible
Orange Financial NFT's do not have to be staked on any smart contracts and can be stored on cold wallets with no connection to the internet.
Diversification of Assets
With reward distribution done directly through our NFT, we able to effectively deliver our rewards without worrying about price impact.
Low Gas Fee Cost
Without having to interact with a smart contract to receive rewards, all the transactions fees are paid for by us.
